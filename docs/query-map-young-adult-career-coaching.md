# Query Map: Young Adult Career Coaching / Mentoring

## Purpose

This document turns a broad topic into search surfaces that can be tested on YouTube, Google Trends, Google Search, Reddit, TikTok, LinkedIn, or other platforms.

## Core topic

Young adult career coaching / mentoring.

## Audience definition

People roughly 18–30 who are:

- entering the workforce
- leaving college
- unsure what career path to pick
- trying to get a first professional job
- feeling behind
- changing early jobs
- trying to understand workplace norms
- looking for mentorship
- overwhelmed by vague advice

## Early hypotheses

These are hypotheses to test, not conclusions.

1. The direct phrase “young adult career coaching” may have less volume than problem-based phrases.
2. Searchers may not know they need coaching; they search symptoms instead.
3. Resume and interview content is likely crowded.
4. Workplace navigation may be less saturated and more distinctive.
5. “I don’t know what career I want” may surface more emotionally honest content.
6. Mentorship content may be sales-heavy or generic.
7. The strongest client wedge may be between tactical job search advice and life-direction coaching.
